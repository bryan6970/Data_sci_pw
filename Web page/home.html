About Us

Welcome to PROJECT GREEN!

We are a dedicated group of Secondary three students from HCI (Hwa Chong Institution) who are passionate about utilizing data science to drive positive change in our country's energy landscape. Our mission is to gather and analyze energy statistics, and develop innovative models and strategies that promote the adoption of green energy sources.

By leveraging the power of data, we aim to contribute towards a sustainable and environmentally conscious future. Through our research and insights, we aspire to empower individuals, communities, and policymakers to make informed decisions that lead to a greener and more sustainable world.

Join us on this journey towards a brighter and cleaner future!
