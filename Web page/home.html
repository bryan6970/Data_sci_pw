About Us

Welcome to PROJECT GREEN!

We are a dedicated group of Secondary three students from HCI (Hwa Chong Institution) who are passionate about utilizing data science to drive positive change in our country's energy landscape. Our mission is to gather and analyze energy statistics, and develop innovative models and strategies that help others with their energy usage.

By leveraging the power of data, we aim to contribute towards a sustainable and environmentally conscious future. Through our research and insights, we aspire to empower individuals, communities, and policymakers to make informed decisions that lead to a greener and more sustainable world.

Join us on this journey towards a brighter and cleaner future!

Our Mission

At Project Green, we aim to drive positive change in our world's energy landscape through the power of data-driven innovation. Comprising a passionate team of dedicated coders, we are committed to leveraging cutting-edge data science techniques to promote the widespread adoption of sustainable energy sources. Through rigorous research, insightful analysis, and collaborative strategies, we aim to empower communities, individuals, and policymakers to make informed decisions that contribute to a greener, more sustainable world. With a strong belief in the potential of data to transform our energy systems, we strive to become a driving force in the promotion of cleaner energy.
